💡 How is this calculated?

We use a simple 3-step magic formula to figure out your costs:

  • Step 1: Distance ÷ Mileage = How many litres you need.
  • Step 2: Litres × Fuel Price = Your total trip cost.
  • Step 3: 30 Days × Daily Commute = Your monthly forecast.
